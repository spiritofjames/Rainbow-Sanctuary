# Group Healing, Client Journey & Earth Healing

## Public pathway

Group Healing is an optional, accessible first experience—not a prerequisite:

`Group Healing ($20, twice monthly) → Spiral I → Spiral II → Spiral III → Spiral IV`

After Spiral II, ReGeneration is an optional five-day retreat branch. Short
courses may be taken in any order; their prerequisites and certification claims
still require confirmation.

## Five adapted Group Healing themes

1. Grounding & Renewal
2. Embodied Flow
3. Emotional Release & Integration
4. Rest & Energy Balance
5. Inner Patterns & Boundaries

These preserve the source material's intended emotional and spiritual direction
without publishing unsupported claims about vaccines, toxins, DNA, cells, organs,
immunity, or scientifically proven distance-energy mechanisms.

## Pricing decision

$20 USD is a credible accessible pilot at the low end of visible public group
healing offers. Public comparisons reviewed on 22 July 2026 included $20, $25,
$40–$60, and €70 offers with different formats and durations. Keep $20 for 6–8
sessions, then review attendance, repeat rate, refunds/no-shows, and movement into
Spiral I. A later three-session pass could be tested at $54 without discounting
the single session below its intended energy exchange.

## Earth Healing Zone

The Earth Healing Zone is separate from the paid Group Healing funnel. It is a
community practice combining a shared recorded or future live meditation with
reflection and tangible ecological stewardship. The existing China-based team is
described as the early community foundation without inventing scale or outcomes.
