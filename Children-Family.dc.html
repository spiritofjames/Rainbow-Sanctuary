What guides the work

The child is never a problem to be fixed.

We begin with curiosity about strengths, needs, context, relationships, and the conditions in which a child feels safe enough to participate.

Choice & consent

Children need age-appropriate choice, clear explanations, and respect for their no.

Strengths before labels

We observe capacities and needs without diagnosing or reducing a child to a difficulty.

Family continuity

Caregivers receive simple language and practices that can support life beyond the session.

Scope & referral

Educational wellbeing support sits alongside—not in place of—qualified clinical or learning care.
