The learning journey

Structured enough to follow. Spacious enough to be personal.

Each part builds from observation into practice, then brings the learning back into everyday life.

01Relationship with place+

Develop observation, gratitude, reciprocity, and a grounded practice of listening to land and community.

02The elemental lens+

Use earth, water, air, fire, and space as reflective frameworks—not substitutes for environmental science.

03Collective field awareness+

Study group dynamics, facilitation agreements, consent, scope, and referral.

04Service design+

Shape a small, responsible project aligned with the Planetary Symbiosis Network vision.

Stephanie’s living-systems framework

Information becomes practice. Practice meets reality.

The work moves through three connected lenses. None stands alone, and each remains open to reflection, evidence, and your own experience.
