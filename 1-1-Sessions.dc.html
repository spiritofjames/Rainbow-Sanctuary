Application-led care

Scheduling begins after fit is confirmed.

Private healing is not an instant-booking service. The review step protects the applicant, the practitioner, and the scope of the work.

01Share your present challenge and intended outcome+

Choose a session and describe what you are navigating in your own words. Please do not upload medical records or unnecessary information about another person.

02Provide a recent headshot with explicit consent+

For private-healing applications, a clear recent image is requested for the stated spiritual review process. It is not used for diagnosis or identity verification. Collection remains disabled until a secure provider, access policy, retention period, and deletion process are confirmed.

03Team review+

The team reviews scope, suitability, consent, and whether another professional or service may be more appropriate. An application is not an acceptance.

04Private scheduling+

If accepted, you receive a private scheduling link. The distance session is arranged for a time when you can rest or sleep, as agreed in advance.

05Written reflection and integration+

After the session, you receive a written reflection describing the practitioner’s process and suggested integration. Any follow-up is discussed separately rather than assumed.

Clear boundaries support deeper work

Spiritual practice is not medical treatment.

Private healing sessions are offered as spiritual and experiential wellbeing practices. They do not diagnose, modify genes, treat illness, guarantee relationship changes, remove legal or financial obligations, or replace medical, psychological, safeguarding, or emergency support.
