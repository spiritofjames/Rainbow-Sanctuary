Pre-launch note

This policy is tailored to the current website build. The operating legal entity, address, governing law, service providers, and dedicated contact details must be confirmed in site-config.js before public data collection or paid enrollment begins.

1. Who is responsible

Rainbow Sanctuary (operating legal entity to be confirmed), trading as Rainbow Sanctuary, is responsible for the personal information described here. Registered address: to be confirmed before launch.

2. What we collect

Information you choose to send

  • your name and email address;
  • the general reason for your enquiry;
  • an optional message;
  • booking, attendance, payment, or account information only if those services are added and explained before collection;
  • communications and preferences when you correspond with us.

Technical information

The production host may create limited security and access logs such as IP address, browser type, requested page, date, and time. The current site does not use analytics, advertising pixels, profiling, or automated decision-making.

Sensitive information

Please do not send medical records, diagnoses, government identifiers, financial details, or unnecessary information about a child through the consultation form. If sensitive information is volunteered, we will limit its use to the enquiry, ask for an appropriate basis where required, or securely remove it when it is not needed.

3. How and why we use information

  • to respond to an enquiry and suggest a suitable next step;
  • to take steps you request before entering an agreement;
  • to arrange and deliver a confirmed service;
  • to maintain site security, prevent misuse, and keep necessary records;
  • to meet legal, safeguarding, accounting, and regulatory duties;
  • to send marketing only when you have made a clear choice to receive it.

Depending on your location and the activity, the legal basis may be consent, steps before or performance of a contract, compliance with law, protection of vital interests, or a legitimate interest that does not override your rights.

4. Who receives information

We do not sell personal information. We may share only what is necessary with confirmed providers that support hosting, form delivery, email, scheduling, payment, accounting, or professional advice; with facilitators involved in a service you request; or when disclosure is required by law or needed to protect someone from serious harm.

The form provider, host, payment provider, countries of processing, and any safeguards for international transfers must be recorded here when selected. The site currently requests fonts from Google Fonts; Google states that its Web Fonts API does not set or log cookies, although the browser sends requests to Google’s font domains.

5. Children’s privacy

This website is for adults and is not designed to collect information directly from children. A parent or legal guardian must make enquiries about a child. Children should not submit the consultation form.

Information about a child is treated as sensitive and collected only when necessary for a specific program, with age-appropriate explanation and the required parent or guardian authorization. Photos, video, or audio of a child require a separate, specific permission process; participation is not conditioned on publicity consent.

6. Retention and security

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed to respond, manage any resulting relationship, meet legal duties, and resolve disputes. The final enquiry retention period is to be confirmed with the selected form and email provider. Records connected to paid services may require a different legally mandated period.

We use proportionate administrative and technical safeguards and limit access to people who need the information. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may be entitled to ask for access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or an objection to certain processing; withdraw consent without affecting earlier lawful use; and complain to your local data-protection authority. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

You can unsubscribe from optional marketing at any time. The current site does not run an email newsletter or behavioral advertising.

8. Contact and changes

Send privacy questions or requests to the privacy contact listed before launch. We will update this policy when the site’s forms, providers, payments, analytics, or services change and will revise the date above.
