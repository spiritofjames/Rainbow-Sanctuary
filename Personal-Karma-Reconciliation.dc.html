The private practice

Reconciliation, not erasure.

The intention is not to deny history or force forgiveness. It is to meet what remains with attention, discernment, compassion, and responsibility.

01Name the repeating pattern+

Clarify what you notice, what it means to you, and what is known versus assumed.

02Explore the emotional and energetic story+

Work with imagery, attention, and Rainbow Sanctuary’s spiritual framework without presenting one interpretation as unquestionable fact.

03Hold accountability and compassion together+

Distinguish responsibility, repair, boundaries, and self-punishment. This session does not replace direct communication, restitution, or legal obligations.

04Choose an integration practice+

Translate any insight into a realistic present-day action, boundary, conversation, or support request.
