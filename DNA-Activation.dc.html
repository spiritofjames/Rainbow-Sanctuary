A necessary distinction

Spiritual metaphor—not genetic intervention.

In this offering, DNA represents inherited stories, latent capacities, identity, and the feeling of carrying a deep inner pattern. The session does not alter, repair, activate, delete, or measure biological DNA, genes, cells, or disease processes.

Family beliefs or survival patterns that continue to shape your choices.

04

Embodied possibility

A desire to experience potential as a lived practice rather than an abstract idea.
